    # Research Experience

    Institute of Television and Image Information, Tianjin University
    2021.09 - 2024.01

    Advised by Dan Songopen in new window.

    1. I participated in scientific research on virtual try-on and designed a model called CAT-DM based on ControNet and PBE. This model utilizes the implicit distribution generated by a pre-trained GAN-based model to initiate the reverse denoising process. Compared to the original PBE model, CAT-DM improved the FID metric by 6.1. Additionally, when compared to the default 50-step sampling of other diffusion models, CAT-DM demonstrated a 25-fold increase in speed.
    2. I participated in scientific research on fashion editing and proposed a novel framework for generating fashion images. This framework allows users to generate customized fashion images by providing editing cues. When evaluated on the DeepFashion dataset, our model demonstrated a 2.1 improvement in the FID metric compared to CocosNet v2.
    3. I am responsible for the daily operation of the servers, proficient in Linux system operation, and proficient in server maintenance skills.
