    👋 Hello! This is Jianhao Zeng (曾建豪).





    I am a Research Assistant in the School of Engineering at Westlake University, supervised by Prof. Guojun Qi. Before that, I graduated from Tianjin University with a B.S. degree in Mechanical Design & Manufacturing and Automation and an M.S. degree in Electronic and Information Engineering, where I was fortunate to be guided by Prof. Dan Song. My research area focuses on Artificial Intelligence Generated Content (AIGC), especially generative models and their application, including video generation, image generation and 3D content generation.

    I am currently preparing to apply for PhD programs starting in the fall of 2025. If there are any opportunities, potential collaborations, or if you'd like to connect and make friends, please feel free to reach out to me.
