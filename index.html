    Hello!

    My name is Jianhao Zeng (曾建豪).

    I am a Research Assistant in the School of Engineering at Westlake University, supervised by Prof. Guojun Qi. Before that, I graduated from Tianjin University with a B.S. degree in Mechanical Design & Manufacturing and Automation and an M.S. degree in Electronic and Information Engineering, where I was fortunate to be guided by Prof. Dan Song. My research area focuses on Artificial Intelligence Generated Content (AIGC), especially in video generation and image generation.

    I am currently preparing to apply for PhD programs starting in the fall of 2025. If there are any open positions or opportunities available, please feel free to contact me.
